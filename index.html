Biography

I received Ph.D degree from Inner Mongolia University, China in 2020 and Bachelor degree in Taiyuan University of Technology, ShanXi, China in 2014. Currently, I am now with the Department of Electrical & Computer Engineering, the National University of Singapore for a research attachment under Prof. Haizhou Li.

My current research interests include Expressive Speech Synthesis. Revolving around this goal, I mainly study the following topics: (1) Machine Learning: End-to-End Text-to-Speech (TTS), Speech Emotion Recognition et. al. (2) Natural Language Processing: Prosody Structure Prediction, Grapheme-to-Phoneme Conversion (G2P), Syntax Parsing et. al.
